Ladder Golf

aka. Bola Golf

Ladder Golf is played with 2 or more players or teams. Each player has 3 golf ball bolas. A bola is 2 golf balls attached by a nylon rope. The object of the game is to wrap your bolas around the steps of the ladder. The ladder consists of 3 steps, a top, middle and a bottom step.

Kan Jam

aka. kanjam, Can, Can Jam, Playin Can

KanJam is a flying disc game, played with a flying disc and two cans in which you deflect the disc. The KanJam company is based in western New York.

Corn Hole

aka. dummy boards, bean bag toss, dadhole, doghouse, Baggo, or simply Bags,

Corn Hole is an American lawn game in which players take turns throwing bags of corn at a raised platform with a hole in the far end. A bag in the hole scores 3 points, while one on the platform scores 1 point. Play continues until a team or player reaches the score of 21.
